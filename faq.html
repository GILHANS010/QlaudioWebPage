Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about QLAUDIO's products and services. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us.

What is QLAUDIO?

QLAUDIO is a company specializing in audio plugin development, web-based DAWs, and various music production tools. We provide cutting-edge technology to enhance your music and sound projects.

How can I purchase QLAUDIO products?

You can purchase our products directly from our website. Simply browse our plugins and services, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

Do you offer free trials?

Yes, we offer free trials for most of our software and plugins. Check the product pages for trial versions and download links.

What is QLAUDIO Connective?

QLAUDIO Connective is a web-based node module, similar to MaxMSP, designed for advanced sound and music production directly in your browser.

How do I get support for QLAUDIO products?

You can get support by visiting our contact page and sending us a message. Our support team is available to assist you with any issues or questions you may have.

Are there any subscription-based services?

Yes, we offer subscription-based services such as Qlaudio Premium Connective and Qlaudio Premium Genesis, which provide advanced features and additional modules.

What is QLAU-DAW?

QLAU-DAW is our web-based digital audio workstation, similar to Logic Pro X, but with integrated community functions for collaboration and sharing.
